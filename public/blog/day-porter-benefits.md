## What Day Porters Do

 
[Day porters](https://rangeljanitorial.com/services/day-porter) handle real-time cleaning needs during business hours including lobby maintenance, restroom monitoring and restocking, break room cleaning, conference room turnover, spill cleanup, and trash management.

 
## Who Benefits Most

 
Facilities with heavy foot traffic benefit the most from day porter services. This includes corporate lobbies, medical offices, fitness centers, retail spaces, and multi-tenant buildings where a single nightly cleaning is not enough.

 
## Day Porter vs Nightly Cleaning

 
Nightly [janitorial cleaning](https://rangeljanitorial.com/services/janitorial-cleaning) provides a thorough deep clean after business hours. Day porter service supplements this by maintaining cleanliness during operating hours. The most effective facility maintenance programs use both services together.
